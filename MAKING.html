MAKING SENSE AND MEANING

What design means for you?

Design is expressing through any technique, tool, or something an option to a solution in that specific time-lapse that is close related to me, sometimes it is not that closely but it became somehow a situation that I love, like, live, and I feel it might work because it works for me due to my perception. In this process I´m trying to have empathy for other humans or non-humans who are part of the experience; making an effort to embodied like them, as well as living all the processes that this involves.

It is a sensibility of how I recognize my environment and how I am motivated to express a new way of how things can be done, or just because of the joyful and amusing expression of my understanding.

It is a step further that satisfies my curiosity and subtle look to the beyond.
